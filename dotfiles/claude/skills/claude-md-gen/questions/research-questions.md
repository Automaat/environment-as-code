## Question Selection Strategy

**Always ask:** Q1, Q2, Q8 (note types, workflow, quality standards are essential)

**Ask if PARA/Zettelkasten detected:** Q6 (organization method details)

**Ask if research-heavy:** Q5, Q7 (templates and citations)

**Ask about AI usage:** Q3 (how AI should assist)

**Total questions:** 5-7 based on project characteristics
